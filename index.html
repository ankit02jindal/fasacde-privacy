Privacy Policy

Fasacde App • Last updated: 15 January 2026

Fasacde (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Fasacde mobile application, which brings artworks and motifs to life using interactive visual experiences.

1. Camera Usage

Fasacde uses your device’s camera solely to:

  • Scan artworks, motifs, or facades
  • Trigger visual or animated experiences

We do not record, store, or transmit camera images or video to our servers.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Fasacde does not collect personal data, including but not limited to:

  • Name, email address, or phone number
  • Location data
  • Contacts or address book
  • Device identifiers for tracking

3. Web Content & External Links

Some features within Fasacde may open external web pages (for example, additional artwork information or interactive experiences).

These external pages are governed by their own privacy policies. Fasacde does not control or store any data from these websites.

4. Local Storage

The app may store minimal local preferences on your device, such as whether onboarding screens have been completed. No sensitive or personal data is stored.

5. Children’s Privacy

Fasacde does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The app is intended for general audiences, including educational and exhibition environments.

6. Security

We take reasonable steps to ensure that:

  • App permissions are used responsibly
  • No personal data is misused or transmitted
  • Interactions remain local to your device whenever possible

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the updated version available through the app or associated website.
